About Us

We're committed to providing authentic wildlife experiences while protecting Tanzania's natural treasures.

Sustainable Tourism Since 2010

Founded by native Tanzanians with a passion for conservation, Serengeti Explorer has been at the forefront of sustainable eco-tourism for over 15 years. Our mission is to showcase the breathtaking beauty of Tanzania's wilderness while ensuring its preservation for generations to come.

We employ local guides, support community initiatives, and donate a portion of every booking to conservation efforts. When you travel with us, you're not just witnessing nature's wonders – you're helping protect them.
